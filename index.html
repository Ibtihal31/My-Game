MY GAME

Challenge your general knowledge by answering the following question in limited time. The steps are:

  • Click on start button to begin the game.
  • You will see a question with four possible answers.
  • You will have 10 second for every question to think and answer
  • Choose the right answer to be awarded with 10 points.
  • Try to answer before the time is up
  • A message will appear in the screen If your answer is correct or incorrect
  • At the end you will see the final score of correct answers
  • Hit the play again if you want to start over. And Good luck .. !
